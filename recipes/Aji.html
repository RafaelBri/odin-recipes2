Ajitsuke Tamago

Ajitsuke Tamago is a delicacy on it's own, but even more so when paired with Ramen!

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of mirin
  • 1/4 cup of sake

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Gently place the eggs in the pot, cover and cook for 6 and a 1/2 minutes.
    Once cooked, immediately take the pot off the stove and place under cold running water. Move the contents to a bowl with ice cubes and water.
    Carefully remove the shells from the eggs!

  2. Mix the soy sauce, sugar, mirin and sake in a sealable bag. Add the eggs. Seal and make sure that the eggs are fully covered. Place in the refrigerator, and marinate for at least 24 hours.
    This dish can be stored in the refrigerator for up to three days.
